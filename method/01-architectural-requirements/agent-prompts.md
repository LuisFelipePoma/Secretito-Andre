# Agent Prompts - Architectural Requirements

## Intake And Sufficiency

```text
Read project-brief.md and 01-architectural-requirements/input.md. Extract goals, scope, stakeholders, significant functionality, constraints, existing context, assumptions, risks, and open questions. Select or confirm rigor and system context. Stop only for missing information that would materially change architecture; do not propose design.
```

## ASRs And QAW-Lite

```text
Classify architecturally significant functionality, quality attributes, constraints, concerns, and assumptions. Refine material quality attributes into six-part scenarios. Prioritize them with a QAW-lite utility tree and expose measurement gaps and tradeoffs.
```

## Generate Driver Draft

```text
Generate or update architecture-drivers.md from approved sources. Use stable IDs, source hashes, measurable scenarios or accepted gaps, primary/supporting drivers, risks, and the phase exit checklist. Keep all architecture choices out of this artifact and leave it Draft for architect review.
```
